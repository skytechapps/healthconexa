Privacy Policy for HealthConexa Last Updated: January 31, 2026 HealthConexa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your health data when you use the HealthConexa mobile application. 1. Information We Collect Health Data via Google Health Connect HealthConexa integrates with Google Health Connect to provide a unified health dashboard. With your explicit permission, we may read the following data types: Activity (Steps, Calories Burned) Vitals (Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, Blood Glucose) Body Measurements (Weight, Height, BMI) Sleep Patterns User-Provided Information We collect information you manually enter into the app, such as manual blood pressure logs, blood sugar readings, or height/weight updates. AI Insights Data When you request AI-driven health insights, anonymized snapshots of your recent health trends (e.g., "7-day step count average") are processed to generate personalized wellness recommendations. 2. How We Use Your Information To Provide Services: Displaying your health metrics in an easy-to-read dashboard. AI Insights: Generating personalized wellness tips using Google's Gemini AI. Local Storage: Storing your data on your device for offline access and historical tracking. 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties We do not sell your health data to third parties. We only share data as necessary to provide app functionality: Google Health Connect: Data is synced between your device and Google's secure health framework. Google Gemini AI: Anonymized health snapshots are sent to generate insights. No personally identifiable information (PII) is included in these AI requests. Google AdMob: We may display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization, but it does not have access to your health data. 4. Data Security and Retention On-Device Storage: Most health data is stored locally on your device. No Cloud Storage: We do not maintain a central database of your personal health metrics on our own servers. Permissions: You can revoke access to Health Connect or any specific data type at any time through your Android System Settings. 5. User Rights You have the right to: Access the health data the app displays. Delete any manual logs within the app. Disconnect the app from Google Health Connect. 6. Children's Privacy HealthConexa is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. 8.
