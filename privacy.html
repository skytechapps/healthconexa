Privacy Policy for HealthConexa

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

HealthConexa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your health data when you use the HealthConexa mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data via Google Health Connect

HealthConexa integrates with Google Health Connect to provide a unified health dashboard. With your explicit permission, we may read the following data types:

  • Activity (steps, calories burned)
  • Vitals (heart rate, blood pressure, blood glucose)
  • Body measurements (weight, height, BMI)
  • Sleep patterns

User-Provided Information

We collect information you manually enter into the app, such as blood pressure logs, blood sugar readings, or height and weight updates.

AI Insights Data

When you request AI-driven health insights, anonymized summaries of recent health trends (for example, a 7-day step average) are processed to generate personalized wellness recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide services: Displaying health metrics in an easy-to-read dashboard.
  • AI insights: Generating personalized wellness tips using Google’s Gemini AI.
  • Local storage: Storing data on your device for offline access and historical tracking.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your health data to third parties. Data is shared only as required to provide app functionality:

  • Google Health Connect: Secure health data framework used for syncing.
  • Google Gemini AI: Receives anonymized health summaries only. No personally identifiable information is shared.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization but does not access health data.

4. Data Security and Retention

  • On-device storage: Most health data remains stored locally on your device.
  • No cloud storage: We do not maintain our own servers for storing personal health metrics.
  • Permissions control: You can revoke Health Connect access at any time through Android system settings.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the health data displayed in the app
  • Delete manually entered logs
  • Disconnect HealthConexa from Google Health Connect

6. Children’s Privacy

HealthConexa is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
